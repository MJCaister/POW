Key Information

Capture

Each POW has their date of capture within their profile. This can help to find out at which battle a soldier was captured at.

Rank

The miltary, navy and airforce have a series of different ranks that a person can have. Finding a person's rank can help give an idea of their role within a unit during the war.

Unit

Every unit had a unique experience during the Second World War. A soldier's experience is often greatly influnced by the unit and the other men within it.
